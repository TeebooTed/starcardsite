Overview

Licensed Star baseball cards were printed and sold in player sets beginning in 1983. After the loss of the NBA license, baseball quickly came to be the primary subject of Star’s cards.

Player sets were printed in sets of nine to eleven cards. Star also produced team sets for the Florida State League and Carolina League in 1988 (minimum 24 cards, some reaching 40), promo cards (50–500 printed, 1 per 5 sets ordered), and advertising cards sent to dealers (40 total from 1988–1992, some laminated).
